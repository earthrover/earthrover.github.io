Data

We use robots to monitor every plant and to weed, feed and tend crops efficiently and precisely. We gather data from the fields and provide analysis and planning tools to help farmers improve their productivity.

Farmers

Automonous Farming doesn't just mean swarms of smart robots tending crops. It also means supporting autonomy for farmers, ensuring that they can own and control their technology and their farm's data. Earth Rover are committed to using technology to empower farmers the world over.

Robots

Our robots have been designed by robotics engineers from the European Mars Rover programme. Robust, light-weight and energy efficient these robots, with on-board AI and precision tools, open up a new era of farming technology.
